Understanding Font Properties

Fonts can greatly impact the readability and appearance of your website. Using different font families and weights adds variety and hierarchy to your content.

Font Families in Action

Headings often use serif fonts like Georgia, while body text usually sticks to sans-serif or monospace for clarity.

Why Font Weight Matters

Font weight controls the thickness of characters. Common values include normal, bold, 400, 600, etc.

Font Style Options

You can apply italic, normal, or oblique styles depending on emphasis or design.
